## Summary

Google released Gemma 4 under Apache 2.0, its most capable open model family with four sizes: E2B, E4B, 26B MoE, and 31B Dense. The 31B model ranks #3 on Arena AI's open-source text leaderboard; the 26B MoE activates only 3.8B parameters at inference. All models support native multimodal inputs (video, images, audio) and 140+ languages, with context windows up to 256K for larger sizes.

## Key Highlights

- Four model sizes: E2B/E4B for mobile/edge (128K context); 26B MoE (3.8B active); 31B Dense (256K context)
- 31B ranks #3 on Arena AI open-source text leaderboard; 26B ranks #6
- "Gemma 4 outcompetes models 20x its size" — strong intelligence-per-parameter efficiency
- Native multimodal: video, images, audio support across all sizes
- 140+ language training; Apache 2.0 license
- Targets deployment across billions of Android devices to workstations

## Why It Matters

Apache 2.0 licensing plus frontier-class efficiency democratizes advanced reasoning across consumer hardware, significantly lowering the bar for deploying sophisticated multimodal AI in production applications.
