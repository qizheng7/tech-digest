## Summary

Ineffable Intelligence, a London-based RL startup founded by former Google DeepMind VP David Silver (AlphaGo, AlphaZero, AlphaStar), announced a record $1.1B seed round at a $5.1B post-money valuation on April 27. The round was co-led by Sequoia and Lightspeed, with Nvidia contributing at least $250M. The company focuses on reinforcement learning — AI that learns from experience without human-labeled data — and is the largest seed round in European history.

## Key Highlights

- $1.1B seed round; $5.1B post-money valuation — largest seed in European startup history
- Co-led by Sequoia Capital and Lightspeed Venture Partners; Nvidia contributed ≥$250M
- Investors include Google, DST Global, Index, EQT, BOND Capital, UK Sovereign AI Fund, British Business Bank
- David Silver: former VP of RL at DeepMind, creator of AlphaGo, AlphaZero, AlphaStar
- Focus: reinforcement learning for AI that learns from experience, not human-annotated data
- Silver committed 100% of personal equity proceeds via Founders Pledge

## Why It Matters

The record seed size and investor lineup signal strong conviction that pure RL — building AI from self-generated experience rather than supervised labels — is the next frontier, and that Europe is developing into a serious hub for foundational AI research.
