## Summary

Google launched Gemini 3.1 Flash TTS in preview across Google AI Studio, Vertex AI, and Google Vids. The model supports 70+ languages with granular natural-language controls for voice style, pace, and delivery via "Audio Tags." It scored an Elo of 1,211 on Artificial Analysis's TTS leaderboard and includes SynthID watermarking in all generated audio for AI provenance tracking.

## Key Highlights

- Elo score of 1,211 on Artificial Analysis TTS leaderboard — Google's most natural/expressive TTS model
- Audio Tags: embed natural language style instructions directly in text for scene direction and vocal control
- 70+ language support with advanced style and accent controls
- Speaker-level specificity with exportable parameters for consistent cross-project voice use
- Available in preview on Google AI Studio, Vertex AI, and Google Vids
- SynthID watermarking on all output to identify AI-generated audio

## Why It Matters

Combining top-tier voice quality with low cost and fine-grained developer controls positions Gemini Flash TTS to displace existing TTS providers for enterprise localization, content creation, and real-time voice agent applications.
