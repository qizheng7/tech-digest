## Summary

Anthropic and Amazon signed a major expansion, securing up to 5 gigawatts of new compute for training and deploying Claude. Amazon invests $5B today with up to $20B more potential, while Anthropic commits over $100B to AWS technologies over the next decade. Trainium2 capacity comes online Q2 2026; Trainium3 follows later in 2026. Anthropic's run-rate revenue has surpassed $30B (up from ~$9B at end of 2025).

## Key Highlights

- 5 GW of new compute capacity secured; nearly 1 GW of combined Trainium2/3 by end of 2026
- Amazon invests $5B now; potential total up to $20B (adds to prior $8B invested)
- Anthropic commits $100B+ to AWS over the next decade
- Run-rate revenue surpassed $30B (from ~$9B end of 2025 — ~3.3x in ~4 months)
- 100,000+ customers already running Claude on Amazon Bedrock
- Full Claude Platform will integrate directly within AWS with unified billing and governance
- Trainium2 comes online Q2 2026; Trainium3 later in 2026

## Why It Matters

The scale of this deal — $100B+ commitment, 5 GW of compute — signals that Anthropic is building infrastructure for a model-as-utility future, while validating custom AI silicon as a cost-effective path to meeting explosive demand.
