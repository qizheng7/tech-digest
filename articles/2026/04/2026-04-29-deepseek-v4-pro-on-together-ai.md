## Summary

Together AI launched DeepSeek-V4 Pro on its platform on April 29, 2026, offering the 1.6T-parameter Mixture-of-Experts model with a 512K-token context window, controllable reasoning modes, and aggressive cached-input pricing. The model features 49B activated parameters, hybrid sparse attention (Compressed Sparse Attention + Heavily Compressed Attention), and three reasoning modes: Non-Think, Think High, and Think Max. Together AI is positioning it for long-context agent workflows like code agents, document intelligence, and research synthesis.

## Key Highlights

- **1.6T total / 49B active parameters** — same MoE architecture as the full DeepSeek-V4 Pro release
- **512K context window** with cached-input pricing at $0.20/M tokens (90% discount vs. standard $2.10/M input)
- **Output pricing: $4.40/M tokens**; serverless and dedicated deployment both available
- **Benchmark performance:** LiveCodeBench 93.5%, GPQA Diamond 90.1%, SWE-bench Verified 80.6%, MRCR 1M 83.5%
- **Three reasoning modes** let users trade cost for depth: Non-Think (fast, cheap), Think High, Think Max

## Why It Matters

Together AI making DeepSeek-V4 Pro accessible with sub-cent cached-input pricing accelerates its adoption for long-context agentic pipelines where repeated context is the norm, putting frontier open-weight reasoning within reach of cost-sensitive enterprise workloads.
