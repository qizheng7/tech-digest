## Summary

OpenAI released three new realtime audio models on May 7, 2026: GPT-Realtime-2, GPT-Realtime-Translate, and GPT-Realtime-Whisper. GPT-Realtime-2 is the first voice model with GPT-5-class reasoning, doubling the context window to 128K tokens and adding parallel tool calls with audible status updates. The two companion models handle live multilingual speech translation (70+ input languages → 13 output) and streaming transcription respectively.

## Key Highlights

- GPT-Realtime-2 supports reasoning effort levels from minimal to `xhigh` and scores 15.2% higher on Big Bench Audio vs GPT-Realtime-1.5
- Context window expanded from 32K to 128K tokens; preambles and parallel tool calls now supported
- GPT-Realtime-Translate covers 70+ input languages and 13 output languages for live speech translation
- GPT-Realtime-Whisper delivers streaming speech-to-text at the speed of conversation
- Pricing: GPT-Realtime-2 at $32/$64 per 1M audio input/output tokens; Translate at $0.034/min; Whisper at $0.017/min
- Available to all paid ChatGPT users; API rollout in phased release for developers

## Why It Matters

Voice is the next competitive frontier — GPT-Realtime-2 brings frontier reasoning into real-time speech, shifting voice AI from a novelty to a viable interface for complex agent workflows.
